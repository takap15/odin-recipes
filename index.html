Cranberry Sauce Recipe

What is cranberry sauce?

Cranberry Sauce is a sauce or relish made out of cranberries, commonly served as a condiment or a side dish with Thanksgiving dinner in North America and Christmas dinner in the UK and Canada.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 4 cups cranberries

Steps

      Rinse the cranberries
      Boil water with sugar
      Add cranberries till they burst
